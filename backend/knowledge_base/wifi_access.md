# Corporate & Guest Wi-Fi Access Guide

## Overview
QuickDesk facilities provide high-speed secure Wi-Fi networks for corporate devices and visiting guests.

## Connecting to Corporate Wi-Fi (`QuickDesk-Secure`)
1. Select the Wi-Fi network **QuickDesk-Secure** on your device.
2. When prompted for security protocol, select **WPA3 Enterprise** or **802.1X EAP**.
3. Enter your corporate email address as the Username and your SSO password.
4. Trust the security certificate `cert.quickdesk.internal` when prompted.

## Guest Wi-Fi (`QuickDesk-Guest`)
1. Select **QuickDesk-Guest**.
2. Open your web browser; you will be automatically redirected to the Guest Captive Portal.
3. Enter your full name, email address, and sponsoring employee's email.
4. Guest passes expire after 24 hours.

## Troubleshooting Wi-Fi Issues
If your device disconnects frequently, forget the network in Wi-Fi settings, restart your network adapter, and reconnect. For persistent issues, visit the IT Walk-up Bar on Floor 3.
