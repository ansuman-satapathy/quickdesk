# Employee Leave Application Process

## Overview
All full-time and part-time employees are entitled to Annual Leave, Sick Leave, and Public Holidays in accordance with QuickDesk HR policies.

## Applying for Leave
1. Log in to the HR Portal at `https://hr.quickdesk.com`.
2. Navigate to **Time Off** > **Request Leave**.
3. Select the Leave Type (Paid Time Off, Sick Leave, Parental Leave, Unpaid Leave).
4. Select the start date and end date.
5. Provide a brief reason for absence (required for leaves exceeding 3 consecutive business days).
6. Click **Submit Request**.

## Approval Timelines & Guidelines
- **Standard PTO**: Must be submitted at least 3 business days in advance.
- **Sick Leave**: Can be logged retroactively on the day of return. A doctor's medical certificate is mandatory for absences longer than 2 consecutive days.
- Manager approvals are processed within 24 hours. You will receive an automated email notification once approved.
