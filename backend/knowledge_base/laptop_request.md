# Hardware Provisioning & Laptop Request Policy

## Overview
QuickDesk provides standardized laptops and workstations to all employees based on job function and department requirements.

## Hardware Options
- **Standard Engineering/Product**: Apple MacBook Pro 14" (M-Series, 32GB RAM) or Dell XPS 15 (32GB RAM).
- **Operations/General Staff**: Apple MacBook Air 13" (16GB RAM) or Lenovo ThinkPad T14 (16GB RAM).

## Requesting Hardware
1. **New Hires**: Laptops are automatically requested by HR during onboarding and delivered on Day 1.
2. **Hardware Refresh**: Employees with hardware older than 24 months are eligible for a full refresh. Request via IT Portal > **Hardware Refresh**.
3. **Damaged / Lost Devices**: Report immediately to IT Security. Broken devices will be repaired or replaced within 24 hours.

## Device Compliance
All company-owned laptops must run corporate Endpoint Protection (CrowdStrike) and Full Disk Encryption (FileVault / BitLocker). Unauthorized software installations are monitored.
