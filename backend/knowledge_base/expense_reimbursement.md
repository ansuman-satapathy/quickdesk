# Expense Reimbursement Policy & Submission

## Overview
Employees incurring valid business-related expenses (travel, software subscriptions, client meals) are eligible for full reimbursement.

## Eligible Expense Categories
- **Travel**: Pre-approved flight tickets, train fares, and ride-shares (Uber/Lyft).
- **Meals**: Client entertainment meals up to $75 per person (itemized receipt required).
- **Software/Peripherals**: Team productivity tools or home office accessories up to $200 per year (requires manager approval).

## How to Submit an Expense Claim
1. Access the Finance Portal (`https://finance.quickdesk.com/expenses`).
2. Click **New Expense Claim**.
3. Upload clear scans or digital receipts for all items.
4. Enter the cost, currency, expense category, and cost center code.
5. Click **Submit for Approval**.

## Processing & Reimbursement Schedule
- Claims must be submitted within 30 days of the transaction date.
- Approved expenses are reimbursed directly to your registered direct deposit bank account on the 15th and 30th of each month.
